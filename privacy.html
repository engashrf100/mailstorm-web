Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MailStorm does not collect, store, or share your personal data on any server.

Your CV is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it.

Your email credentials are never accessed by MailStorm. Emails are sent through your own Gmail or Outlook account via official OAuth — we never see or store your password.

Your emails are composed and sent directly from your account. We do not read, store, or share the content of any email you send.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

No account registration is required to use MailStorm. No data is transmitted to our servers at any point.
